What We Make

Our Products

Precision-engineered electroplating jigs and accessories, built to your drawings and specifications.

Electro Plating Jigs

Precision-built holding jigs for electroplating lines, engineered for secure part contact, consistent current distribution and easy loading & unloading.

  • Single Bar Jigs — compact jigs for small to medium components
  • Rack Jigs — multi-tier racks for high-volume plating runs

Painting Jigs

Custom fixtures for paint-line applications with maximum precision, holding parts securely for uniform coverage.

Electro Plating Jigs with Custom Anodes

Integrated jig-and-anode assemblies engineered together for applications needing tightly controlled current distribution across complex part geometries.

Electro Less Plating Jigs

Specialized jigs for electroless (autocatalytic) plating processes, designed to hold components securely through chemical immersion without relying on external current.

Anodes

Custom-fabricated anodes matched to your plating chemistry and tank geometry, for even metal deposition and longer bath life.

PVC Coated Baskets

Corrosion-resistant PVC-coated baskets for bulk plating of small components, protecting bath chemistry from bare metal contact.

PVC Coated Separators

PVC-coated separator plates and dividers that keep components isolated within jigs and baskets during the plating process.
