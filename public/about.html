Our Story

Built on Precision

Sakthi Enterprises has been manufacturing precision jigs, racks, anodes and plating accessories for Bangalore's electroplating and metal finishing industry since 1997. We work directly from customer drawings and specifications to build fixtures that hold tight tolerances and perform reliably on the plating line, cycle after cycle.
